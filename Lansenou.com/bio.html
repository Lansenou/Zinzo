ABOUT ME

Lansenou is a student, gamer and programmer with a passion for computers.

 

The 20 year old is currently in his final year of Game Development at the Hogeschool van Amsterdam. Here he learns what games are and how to build them.

 

In his free time he enjoys programming, drinking beer, playing games, drinking beer and going out. When he's not drinking beer he also likes to play football, after which, ofcourse, he drinks beer.
