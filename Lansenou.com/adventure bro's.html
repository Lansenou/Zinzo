Adventure Bro's

 

From 3 Gamejams we choses the game where the theme was 'Casually Connected' to build upon. This was the first time we used Unity3D and Git.

 

In this game we made a tutorial. I was really happy with the typewriter. It looked fancy to me.

 

The first mechanic of the game was connection/disconnecting with your partner to get more speed and to solve puzzles.

 

Furthermore the target of our game was to fight your way through the level while solving puzzles.

 

Shooting was the secondy mechanic and I was really proud of the gun system, where you were able to switch between the guns and pick them and ammo up. These days I have however learned how this design smells.
